Welcome to CVHS ECO Club!

We are a passionate group of students dedicated to promoting environmental awareness and appreciation for the great outdoors. Our club aims to provide a refreshing escape from the pressures of academics, offering opportunities to connect with nature and communities through various environmental and conservation service projects.

Whether you're an outdoor enthusiast or simply curious about sustainability, we invite you to join us in making a positive impact on our school and surrounding environment. Together, we can explore state parks, engage in community volunteer activities, learn about eco-friendly practices, and create lasting memories.

Join us in our mission to foster a deeper connection with nature and make a difference in the world around us!
